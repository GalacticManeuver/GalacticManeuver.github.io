Rule Changes:

Range modifiers changed to give +1 attack die to the attacker at the closest range a weapon has, and +1 defense die to the defender at the farthest range a weapon has. Only missiles and torpedoes are exempt.

When flying over and shooting through multiple Obstacles, they all take effect.
