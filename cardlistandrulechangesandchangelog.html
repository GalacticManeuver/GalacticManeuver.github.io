Rule Changes:

Range modifiers changed to give +1 attack die to the attacker at the closest range a weapon has, and +1 defense die to the defender at the farthest range a weapon has. Only missiles and torpedoes are exempt.

When flying over and shooting through multiple Obstacles, they all take effect.

Tractor beam tokens changed to Each tractor beam token a ship has, reduces it's agility by 1, to a minimum of 0. In addition, for each tractor beam token a ship has, reduce it's preformed maneuver by 1 to a minimum of 1, the color and type remains the same, even if said maneuver isn't on the ships dial. At the end of the movement phase remove all tractor beam tokens.
